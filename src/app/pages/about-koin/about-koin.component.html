Koin

PeoplePower   : A representation of Public, Permissionless BlockChain.
Koin                  : CryptoCurrency based on PeoplePower.
                   PeoplePower is built on NodeJs, Angular & currently hosted on Github. It is an attempt to understand & visualize processes in a basic BlockChain, such as Cryptography, Consensus, Proof of Work, Transactions, Block Mining, Difficulty, Creation of CryptoCurrency(Mining Reward).
How to use Koin ?
Step 1: Create your Wallet by clicking on the link I want a new Wallet @Create My Wallet
Step 2: Use your Wallet Keys to Access your Wallet.
Step 3: Make a Transaction to Someone you want.
Step 4: Earn Mining Reward by Mining Pending Transactions into a Block.
Step 5: Home Page displays all Blocks Mined in an order of Newest to Oldest.(Latest Mined Block at top)
Step 6: You can check your Wallet Balance in 'Wallet Balance' section.
Step 7: You can Log out from your wallet by clicking on 'Log Out' Button.
Specifications of Koin :
1) Mining Reward:
    - 10 Koins. (Default)
    - Mining Reward will be halved for every 10 Blocks mined.
2) Difficulty:
    - 1 Leading Zero. (Default)
    - As currently PeoplePower is hosted on Server, increasing the Difficulty won't affect Block Mining process that much.
Future Scope :
1) Building a P2P network and use Koin as CryptoCurrency.
2) Increasing the Number of Blocks required to reduce Mining Reward to half of current value.
3) Assign the Difficulty of Mining a Block to Computational Power of P2P Network at any Instance.
    - Ideally Difficulty of Mining a Block is Directly Proportional to Computational Power of P2P Network at any Instance
4) Introduce Smart Contracts on PeoplePower.
